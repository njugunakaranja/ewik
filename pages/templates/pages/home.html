About Us

E-waste Initiative Kenya (EWIK) is a registered NGO founded in 2012, officially recognized in 2016. Headquartered in Gitithia, Kiambu, with branches in Nairobi, we specialize in comprehensive electronic waste management.

Our services include training, collection, processing, and safe disposal, while promoting sustainability and youth empowerment. We aim to create a circular economy by addressing e-waste challenges in Kenya and beyond.
